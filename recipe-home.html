The Best Potato Recipes of All Time!

While many people think of potatoes as a plain and boring food, in fact they are extremely versatile! If prepared well, they can be sweet or savioury, soft, chewy or crunchy and anything inbetween a hearty comfort food and a fancy, sophisticated, restaurant style dish. Here we bring you the very best potato recipes from all around the world, so that you can start your own journey exploring just what potato is capable of!

Please note that this is an ever expanding list of recipes, so do check back in the future to see if there's anything new for you to try!
