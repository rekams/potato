Slambuc

Tastiness rating: 4/5

Difficulty: Advanced

Cooking time: 2 hours and 20 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 3 large potatoes
  • 250g maltagliati pasta
  • 2 onions
  • paprika
  • ground cumin
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 150 gramms of chorizo

  1. Peel the potatoes and chop them into larger, bitesize pieces.
  2. Chop up the onions and the chorizo.
  3. In a large pan, fry up the chorizo.
  4. Add the onions and fry them until glassy. Add some extra cooking oil if necessary.
  5. Once the onion is glassy, remove the pot from the heat and season with a tablespoon of paprika, a teaspoon of cumin and black pepper to taste.
  6. Add the potato chunks, and fry them for a minute or two
  7. Fill the pot with water, until the potatoes are completely covered, and the waterline is 2 or 3 centimeters above the potatoes.
  8. When the potatoes are beginning to soften, add the pasta, the three cloves of chopped garlic and salt to taste.
  9. Once the pasta is properly cooked, remove from the heat and serve!
