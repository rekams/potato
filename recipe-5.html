Dauphinois Potatoes

Tastiness rating: 5/5

Difficulty: Hard

Cooking time: 35 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 7g butter
  • 680g potato, peeled and thinly sliced
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 garlic clove
  • ground nutmeg
  • 50g mature cheddar cheese, grated
  • 150ml double cream

  1. Par-boil the potatoes in boiling water for 4 minutes, until just tender.
  2. Arrange a layer of potato slices in a greased 1.1 litre ovenproof dish. Season lightly, add a little garlic, nutmeg and cheese.
  3. Repeat, then pour cream over the potatoes.
  4. Dot with butter and bake at 190 C for 30 minutes until golden.
