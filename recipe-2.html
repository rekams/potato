Roasted Sweet Potato

Tastiness rating: 5/5

Difficulty: Easy

Cooking time: 1 hour

Ingredients:

  • 1 large sweet potato
  • Olive oil
  • Paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

  1. Preheat your oven to 180˚C.
  2. Take your sweet potato and cut it into 2-3 cm thick slices/chunks.
  3. Place your chunks on a baking tray covered with baking paper.
  4. Generously rub the tops and bottoms of the slices in olive oil. Since we are baking them with their skin on there is no need to rub their sides too.
  5. Season your slices liberally with salt, pepper and paprika
  6. Place your baking tray in the oven for 45 minutes. The potatoes should be ready when they feel like warm butter if poked with a fork, and the skins are becoming detached from the flesh of the potatoes.
  7. After removal from the oven, let the potatoes cool for ten minutes. If desired, the skin can easily be removed at this stage, previous to plating.
