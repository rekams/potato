Potato Wedges

Tastiness rating: 3/5

Difficulty: Easy

Cooking time: 45 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 600g Baking Potatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

  1. Preheat your oven to 200˚C. Put a large pan of salted water on to boil.
  2. Scrub the potatoes clean and get rid of any gnarly bits. Cut the potatoes into chunky wedges. Add to the pan of boiling water and boil for 8 minutes. Drain in a colander and leave to steam dry for a couple of minutes.
  3. Transfer to a roasting tray and add a good lug of olive oil and a pinch of salt and pepper. Toss together so all the wedges are coated in the oil then spread out in one layer. Cook in the hot oven for 30 minutes until golden, crisp and cooked through.
